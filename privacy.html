We do NOT collect, store, or sell your personal data. All screen tests and report generations happen locally on your device.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, IP address, or location.

The extension runs entirely within your browser. When you generate a "Screen Quality Report," the data (such as dead pixel count) is processed temporarily in your browser's local memory and is not sent to any external server.

2. Permissions

We request the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to function:

  • Storage: Used to save your user preferences (e.g., Auto/Manual mode settings) locally.
  • ActiveTab: Used to open the full-screen test page when you click the extension icon.

3. Third-Party Services

The extension may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., Amazon) for product recommendations. If you click on these links, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.
