Tired of Dead Pixels and Backlight Bleeding? How to Choose the Perfect Monitor in 2025

If your old screen just failed our online screen test or you're looking to upgrade your desktop experience, congratulations - the monitor market offers better value than ever before.

But before you place that order, you need to understand a few key parameters to avoid buying another "lemon."

1. Panel Types: IPS vs VA vs OLED

This is the most critical factor determining image quality:

  • IPS Panel (Most Recommended): Most accurate colors, excellent viewing angles. If you're a designer, programmer, or regular office user, go with IPS without hesitation.
    • Drawback: Slight "IPS glow" (blacks aren't perfectly black), which is a normal physical characteristic.
  • VA Panel: High contrast, deep blacks, great for movies. But slower response times may cause ghosting in games.
  • OLED Panel: Currently the top-tier image quality, true pure blacks, extremely fast response.
    • Drawback: More expensive, risk of "burn-in" with long-term use.

2. The Perfect Match: Resolution & Size

Don't just look at resolution - look at pixel density (PPI):

  • 24-inch: Recommend 1080P (budget-friendly choice).
  • 27-inch: 2K (1440P) is the current gold standard - sharp text, reasonable GPU demands.
  • 32-inch and above: Must go with 4K, otherwise you'll get pixelation.

3. Refresh Rate: Not Just for Gaming

Even if you don't play CS:GO or Valorant, we strongly recommend buying a high-refresh-rate monitor of 120Hz or 144Hz.

High refresh rates make mouse movement and web scrolling buttery smooth - once you try it, you can't go back.
